Confluence Scoring

The confluence score ranges from 0 to 5+. Each of the following conditions adds to the score:

Confluence Factors
Active Order Block+1Price is within an active (unmitigated) bullish or bearish OB.
FVG Present+1An unfilled FVG overlaps with the current price zone.
Kill Zone Active+1Current time is within London KZ, NY KZ, or Silver Bullet window.
HTF Bias Aligned+1TF2 and TF3 bias matches the direction of the setup.
NWOG / NDOG Proximity+1Price is within or approaching an unfilled weekly or daily gap level.

A score of 3+ is a tradeable setup. A score of 4‚Äì5 is a high-conviction ICT setup. Use the confluence threshold alert to be notified automatically when conditions align.

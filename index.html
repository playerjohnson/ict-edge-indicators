Frequently Asked Questions

What is ICT trading methodology?
ICT (Inner Circle Trader) methodology is a framework for understanding institutional price delivery. It focuses on concepts like order blocks, fair value gaps, liquidity pools, dealing ranges, and market structure to identify where smart money is positioned and where price is likely to move next.
Do I need a TradingView subscription?
Yes. Our indicators run on TradingView. A free TradingView account works for basic use, but a paid plan (Essential or higher) is recommended to use multiple indicators simultaneously and access real-time data.
Do the indicators repaint?
No. All levels, zones and signals are plotted in real-time and never change on historical bars. What you see in backtesting is exactly what you'd see live.
Which markets do they work on?
ICT Edge indicators work on any market available on TradingView ‚Äî forex, indices, crypto, commodities, stocks. ICT methodology is market-agnostic; wherever there are institutional participants, these concepts apply.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel anytime from your account and you'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. Annual subscribers can request a prorated refund within the first 30 days.
What's the Confluence Suite?
The Confluence Suite (Elite only) merges all five indicators into a single optimised script. It includes a real-time confluence score ‚Äî when multiple ICT concepts align at the same level, the score increases, helping you identify the highest-probability setups.
Is this a signal service?
No. ICT Edge provides tools, not signals. The indicators automate the identification of ICT concepts on your charts, but trading decisions are yours. We believe in giving traders better tools, not telling them what to do.

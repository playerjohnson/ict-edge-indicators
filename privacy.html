Privacy Policy

Effective: 14 February 2026

What We Collect

When you join our waitlist, we collect your email address. When you subscribe, we collect your name, email, billing information and TradingView username. We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site — this collects anonymised usage data including pages visited, time on site and device type.

How We Use It

Your email: to send launch updates, product news and occasional educational content. Your billing info: to process payments via our payment provider. Your TradingView username: to grant access to invite-only indicators. Analytics data: to improve the site and understand which content is useful.

Third Parties

We use Google Analytics (GA4) for site analytics, Google AdSense for advertising, and a payment processor for subscriptions. We do not sell your personal data to anyone, ever.

Cookies

Google Analytics and AdSense set cookies to function. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct or delete your personal data at any time. Email us and we will action any request within 30 days. You can unsubscribe from emails via the link in any email.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us via the email provided in your subscription confirmation.
